About Myself

Nice to meet you!

I'm a freshman studying Environmental Engineering and Computer Science at Cornell University. I'm originally from South Korea but have lived in Indonesia, Canada, Washinton D.C., and Hawaii. While living in Hawaii, I did a native Hawaiian plant conservation project which was the official beginning of my interest in sustainability.

I'm interested in applying computer programming for environmental sustainability. Through clubs, on-campus organizations, and coursework, I hope to be able to achieve my academic and career goals.
